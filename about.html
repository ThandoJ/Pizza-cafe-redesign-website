Our Story

From a tiny oven to a neighbourhood favourite — we believe in simple ingredients, bold flavour, and a warm welcome.

Pizza Cafe started as a school-project dream and evolved into a community spot where friends and families gather for great pizza. Our dough is made fresh daily and each pizza is fired to perfection.

  • Fresh locally sourced toppings
  • Hand-stretched dough
